folksay Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

folksay is a simple sayings and proverbs app. We do not sell personal data.

Data we store

The app may store your selected language, saved sayings, notification preference, and local background image choices on your device so the app works as expected.

Photos

If you choose a photo as a sharing background, the app asks for access to your photo library. The selected image is used locally for the sharing feature and is not uploaded by us.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notification settings are used to show local reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in the app or in your device settings.

Third parties

Sharing actions are handled by your device and the app or service you choose to share with. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.
